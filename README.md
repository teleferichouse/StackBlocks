# Privacy Policy for Stack Clash

**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Stack Clash ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game Stack Clash (the "Game"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

By downloading, installing, or playing Stack Clash, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not download or use the Game.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Automatically Collected

When you play Stack Clash, we automatically collect certain information about your device and gameplay:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Gameplay Data:** Game progress, high scores, gameplay statistics, and session duration
- **Technical Data:** Crash reports, performance data, and diagnostic information

### 2.2 Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

- **High Score:** Your highest score achieved in the game
- **Game Progress:** Number of games played and gameplay statistics

### 2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Photos or media files
- Financial information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Game Functionality:** To provide core game features and save your progress
- **Performance Improvement:** To analyze game performance and fix bugs
- **Analytics:** To understand how players interact with the game and improve gameplay experience
- **Advertising:** To display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance

## 4. Third-Party Services

Stack Clash integrates the following third-party services that may collect information:

### 4.1 Unity Technologies

**Service:** Unity Analytics and Unity Ads  
**Purpose:** Game analytics and in-game advertising  
**Data Collected:** Device identifiers, gameplay data, ad interaction data  
**Privacy Policy:** [https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy)

### 4.2 Google AdMob

**Service:** Mobile advertising  
**Purpose:** Display advertisements within the game  
**Data Collected:** Device identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location (for relevant ads)  
**Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)  
**Opt-Out:** You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
- **iOS:** Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
- **Android:** Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

### 4.3 Unity In-App Purchasing (IAP)

**Service:** In-game purchase processing  
**Purpose:** Process in-game transactions (if implemented)  
**Data Collected:** Purchase information is handled by your device's app store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store)  
**Note:** We do not collect or store payment information directly

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

- **Third-Party Service Providers:** With Unity Technologies and Google AdMob as described above
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## 6. Children's Privacy

Stack Clash is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The game is designed to be COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) compliant.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at the email below, and we will take steps to remove such information.

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Stored on your device until you uninstall the game or clear app data
- **Analytics Data:** Retained by third-party services according to their privacy policies
- **No Server Data:** We do not maintain user accounts or server-side data storage

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access:** Request information about data collected from your gameplay
- **Deletion:** Uninstall the game to remove all locally stored data
- **Opt-Out of Ads:** Adjust ad personalization settings on your device (see Section 4.2)
- **Analytics Opt-Out:** Disable analytics through your device's privacy settings

Stack Clash may be played worldwide. Information collected may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

By using the Game, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.
